The Story Behind
Our Wine

Located in the Shenandoah Valley of Amador County, our tasting room overlooks the panoramic views of the Cosumnes River Canyon amidst a 120 year old vineyard and 300 year old oaks. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will guide you through tasting options while sharing stories of the wines, vineyards and the events of our 120-year history


Our tasting room and picnic grounds are open daily. Whether you’re looking for great wine, the perfect picnic location, or just a quiet spot to commune with nature, you’ll find it here at Story Winery. See you soon!
